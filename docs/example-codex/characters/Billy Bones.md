# Billy Bones

**Who they are:** An old pirate, formerly Flint's first mate, hiding at the Admiral
Benbow inn with Flint's map locked in his sea chest. Called only "the captain" for
most of his page time.

## Facts (canon, with chapter of establishment)
- Appearance: tall, strong, heavy; tarry pigtail over a soiled blue coat; hands
  ragged and scarred; a sabre cut, "dirty, livid white," across one cheek (Ch 1).
- Sings "Fifteen men on the dead man's chest"; drinks rum and water nightly (Ch 1).
- Pays Jim fourpence a month to watch for a one-legged seafaring man (Ch 1).
- Owns a sea chest he never opens in company (Ch 1); admits the gang is "after my
  sea chest" (Ch 2).
- Suffers a first stroke during the Black Dog fight (Ch 2); warned a second will
  kill him (Ch 2); dies on receiving the black spot (Ch 3).
- Name "Billy Bones" used by Black Dog ("my mate Bill", Ch 2) and confirmed in
  the chest's papers (Ch 4).

## Arc involvement
- [[Arc - The Old Buccaneer]]: the man the whole arc chases; his death hands the
  map to Jim.

## Voice notes
- Roars and bullies company; calls Jim "sonny". Talks of the sea only in threats
  and songs, never plainly.

## Open questions
- (resolved by death; what he did to earn the spot is told only in fragments)
