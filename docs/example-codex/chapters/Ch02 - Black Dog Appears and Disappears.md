# Ch 2 - Black Dog Appears and Disappears

**Logline:** A pale stranger missing two fingers corners the captain at breakfast;
the meeting ends in a cutlass fight, and the captain collapses with a stroke.

## Events (in order)
1. Early January morning; the captain walks out to the cove with his brass
   telescope.
2. A stranger enters the inn: pale, tallowy, two fingers missing from his left
   hand, wearing a cutlass. He asks for "my mate Bill" and waits with Jim.
3. The captain returns; the stranger names himself Black Dog. The captain goes
   white; the two close the door for a private talk; Jim is sent away but
   overhears rising voices.
4. The talk breaks into a fight; cutlasses; Black Dog flees wounded in the
   shoulder, the captain pursuing as far as the door.
5. The captain collapses: a stroke. Dr. Livesey (visiting Jim's father) bleeds
   him, names the rum as the cause, and warns that another stroke will kill him.
6. The captain wakes; he tells Jim he must get away from here, that "they're
   after my sea chest," and that Black Dog's people want it.
