# Arc - The Old Buccaneer

**What it is:** The opening movement. A dying pirate hides at a country inn with the
key to Flint's treasure in his sea chest; his old crew hunts him down, and the map
passes to Jim and the gentry, launching the voyage.

## Spine (beat by beat)
- [[Ch01 - The Old Sea-Dog at the Admiral Benbow]]: the captain lodges at the inn; the watch for the one-legged man begins.
- [[Ch02 - Black Dog Appears and Disappears]]: the crew makes first contact; the captain's first stroke.
- Ch03: the black spot sets a deadline ("ten o'clock"); the captain dies; Jim's father has died.
- Ch04: Jim and his mother open the chest; they take the money owed and the oilskin packet.
- Ch05: the gang ransacks the inn for the packet; Pew is trampled to death.
- Ch06: Livesey and Trelawney open the packet: Flint's map. The voyage is resolved; Jim will sail as cabin boy.

## Status
RESOLVED as a chase for the chest; it hands the map and the unresolved threat
(the rest of the crew, the one-legged man) to the voyage that follows.
